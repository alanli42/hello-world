UI/UX Design Master Thesis

In order to evaluate the impact of combining Social media to Virtual Learning Environments for the purposes of increasing student engagement, a deductive and inductive research was used by conducting focus group discussions, interviews and test sessions. A comparative study has been investigated between Social media (Facebook) and VLEs (Solent Online Learning). The study investigated the strength and weaknesses of both Facebook and Solent Online Learning, the problems were found then the new design was implemented according to the results and findings of focus group discussions. Finally, the aim and objectives has been achieved with the results of the testing sessions by showing the effectiveness of combining social media to Virtual Learning Environments for increasing the student engagements.
